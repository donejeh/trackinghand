Hand gestures
· Spread fingers — expand (stronger hand wins; both hands widen more)
· Move hand(s) left/right — shift colors (average if two hands)
· Second hand — palm separation adds extra scale; palm X nudges spin
· Pinch on first detected hand — next / hold for previous
· D — toggle landmark debug overlay on the camera preview
· Camera preview — drag its bottom-right corner to resize; double-click the preview for small / medium / large
